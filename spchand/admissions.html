Admissions 2026–27

Registration is open for Nursery through Class IX, with limited seats in the senior secondary streams.

How to Apply

Four steps, no agents, no capitation

The entire process runs through the school office. We do not work with admission consultants, and there is no charge beyond the published registration fee.

1. Enquire

Call the office or submit the enquiry form. We'll confirm seat availability for the class you want.

2. Visit & Collect

Visit the campus during a working day and collect the registration form from the front office.

3. Submit & Interact

Submit the completed form with documents. A short interaction is scheduled with the child and parents.

4. Confirm

On selection, deposit the admission fee within the stated window to confirm the seat.

Documents Required

What to bring

  • Birth certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Transfer Certificate from the previous school, if applicable
  • Report card / mark sheet of the last class attended
  • Aadhaar card of the student and both parents
  • Four recent passport-size photographs
  • Address proof and, where applicable, category certificate

Scholarships & Concessions

If the fee is the only obstacle, talk to us

Every year we set aside a portion of seats for merit and need-based support. Applications are reviewed confidentially by the Principal and the school committee.

  • Merit scholarship — top performers in each grade, reviewed annually
  • Need-based concession — partial or full tuition waiver
  • Sibling concession — for families with more than one child enrolled
  • Single-parent & service-family support — case-by-case

FAQ

Common questions

What is the minimum age for Nursery?

The child should have completed 3 years as on 31 March of the admission year.

Is there an entrance test?

For Nursery and KG there is only an informal interaction. From Class I upwards there is a short written assessment in English, Mathematics and the child's second language.

Are mid-session admissions possible?

Yes, subject to seat availability and a valid Transfer Certificate — most commonly for families relocating.

Which board does the school follow?

CBSE. Affiliation details are available on request at the school office.

Does the school provide transport?

Yes, on published routes. Charges depend on distance and are billed separately from tuition.

Ready to start?

Send an enquiry and the office will get back to you with seat availability and the next available visit slot.
