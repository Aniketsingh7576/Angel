Our Focus

An education that reaches further than the exam hall

Strong fundamentals in language and mathematics, real laboratory science, digital literacy from the primary years, and a daily culture of respect. These are the four things we refuse to compromise on.

Academic Rigour

A structured, concept-first curriculum with continuous assessment rather than last-minute cramming.

Values & Character

Raising citizens, not just candidates

Honesty is enforced in the examination hall and expected in the corridor. Every student belongs to a house, every house takes on a service project, and every child is expected to speak up, listen properly, and disagree well.

  • Daily morning assembly with student-led thought for the day
  • Four-house system running academics, sport and culture
  • Annual community service and cleanliness drives
  • Anti-bullying policy with a named teacher for every grade

Fees & Support

A good school should not be out of reach

Our fees are kept deliberately modest and are published in full — no surprise charges mid-session. Merit and need-based concessions are available every year, and no family is asked to explain themselves twice.

  • Full fee structure published before admissions open
  • Merit scholarships for top performers in each grade
  • Need-based fee concession, reviewed confidentially
  • Instalment options across the academic session
