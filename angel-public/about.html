About Angel Public School

A co-educational school for Nursery through Class XII, built around teaching done properly and children treated well.

Angel Public School was founded to give families in and around Mundera Chand a genuine alternative: a school small enough that every teacher knows every child by name, and serious enough that no one has to leave for tuition.

Vision

To be the school our community trusts first — a place where academic seriousness and personal kindness are treated as the same discipline, not competing ones.

Mission

  • Teach every subject to understanding, not to memorisation.
  • Keep class sizes small enough for individual attention to be real.
  • Give every child timetabled access to sport, art and the laboratory.
  • Hold students, staff and ourselves to one standard of honesty.
  • Keep fees affordable and fully transparent.

Principal's Message

“Every child is already capable. Our job is not to get in the way.”

Parents ask me what makes a school good. My answer has not changed in all my years of teaching: it is not the building, and it is not the board results. It is whether a child feels safe enough to admit they did not understand something — and whether a teacher is patient enough to explain it again.

At Angel Public School we take the syllabus seriously and we take the child more seriously. Our teachers stay back when a student is behind. Our examination hall is honest. Our ground is used every single day. If that is the kind of school you want for your child, come and see an ordinary working day here — not a rehearsed tour.

Santosh Chand Ji
Principal, Angel Public School

Our People

The faculty

Over thirty qualified teachers across the primary, middle and senior wings, supported by laboratory assistants, a librarian, a counsellor and administrative staff.

Primary Wing

Class teachers for Nursery–V, with specialist teachers for music, art and physical education.

Middle Wing

Subject teaching begins in Class VI, with dedicated language, mathematics and science faculty.

Senior Wing

Postgraduate subject specialists for Classes IX–XII across science, commerce and humanities.

Support Staff

Counsellor, librarian, lab assistants, transport supervisors and front-office team.

Come and see the school

We do not do staged tours. Call ahead, pick a school day, and walk the corridors while class is on.
